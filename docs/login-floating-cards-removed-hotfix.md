# Login Floating Cards Removed Hotfix

## Purpose

The animated floating cards on the glassmorphism login screen were visually overlapping and hindering the background showcase content on wider screens.

## Change

This hotfix removes the extra floating flyer cards from the signed-out login screen while preserving the main glassmorphism layout, animated aurora background, route accent, SaaS snapshot card, login/signup/forgot-password flows, and mobile responsiveness.

## Result

- No floating cards overlap the login showcase.
- Main headline, snapshot, and login form remain readable.
- Login screen stays animated through background aurora, route pulse, card fade-in, and button shimmer.
- No backend, database, or environment variable changes are required.
